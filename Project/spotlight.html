Ed Sheeran

Ed Sheeran is a British singer-songwriter born on February 17, 1991, in Halifax, West Yorkshire, England. He began playing guitar at a young age and started writing songs while still in high school. Sheeran moved to London in 2008 to pursue a music career and started performing in small venues around the city. He gained popularity through social media and released his debut album "+ (Plus)" in 2011, which included hit singles like "The A Team" and "Lego House." His second album "x (Multiply)" was released in 2014 and included chart-topping hits like "Sing," "Thinking Out Loud," and "Photograph."
